Easy, cheesy Pull Apart Garlic Bread

This is a simple and quick recipe for the most delicious garlic bread you've ever tasted!

Ingredients

  • 1 loaf Italian bread
  • 1/2 cup butter, melted
  • 1 to 2 cloves garlic, finely chopped
  • 1/2 cup shredded mozzarella cheese
  • 1/2 cup shredded Cheddar cheese

Directions

  1. Preheat oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C).
  2. Cut the bread into 1 inch slices without cutting all the way through the bottom crust. Place slices on a foil-lined baking sheet.
  3. In a small bowl, stir together melted butter and garlic. Spoon mixture evenly over bread, making sure it's getting in between the slices.
  4. Combine cheeses in a small bowl; sprinkle evenly between bread slices, again getting in between each slice.
  5. Bake in preheated oven for 10 to 15 minutes, until cheese is melted and bread is lightly toasted. Serve immediately.
